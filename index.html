Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new entry?

Click the "New Entry" button in the toolbar or use ⌘N.

Are my entries secure?

Your entries are stored locally and protected by macOS security.

How do I change themes?

Select between Freeform and Three Things modes in the entry editor.

Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. Here's how we protect your data:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No personal information is collected
  • Your entries are never uploaded to any servers
  • Protected by macOS security features
